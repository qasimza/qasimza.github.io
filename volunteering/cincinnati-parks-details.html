Volunteering

  • Organization: Cincinnati Parks, Clean Up Cincinnati
  • Position: Recycling and Beautification Volunteer
  • Dates: Jan 2020 - Feb 2023

As a volunteer for Cincinnati Parks and Clean Up Cincinnati, I took an active role in promoting sustainability and environmental conservation. I led monthly recycling efforts, collaborating with the community to achieve our sustainability goals. Additionally, I participated in various conservation, management, and beautification projects in Cincinnati parks, working to preserve and enhance our natural spaces for the community to enjoy. These experiences not only allowed me to contribute positively to the environment but also taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, leadership, and the importance of environmental stewardship.
