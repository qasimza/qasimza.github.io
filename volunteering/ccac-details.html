Volunteering

  • Organization: Clifton Center of Culture and Arts
  • Position: Volunteer Organizer
  • Dates: Jan 2019 - May 2019

As a volunteer for Clifton Center of Culture and Arts (CCAC), I played a key role in coordinating community engagement events throughout the summer following COVID-19. This involved weekly activities such as setting up and tearing down event venues, as well as handling various tasks during events, including conducting art workshops for children aged 3 to 11, managing check-ins, and assisting with other event-related duties. My involvement with CCAC allowed me to make a meaningful contribution to emotional welfare of the local community in the post-pandemic season.
