The University Honors Program (UHP) at the University of Cincinnati is composed of undergraduate students who rank in the top 7% of the university. This program fosters an engaging environment that challenges students to pursue excellence and equip themselves with the skill set to lead impactful changes in the world. As a part of this program, I completed 5 different honors experiences which have diversified my knowledge beyond my major in a variety of areas ranging from the realm of energy, environment and society, to education, politics and socially conscientious game design.
